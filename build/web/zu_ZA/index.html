Blokada, the ad blocker

Uma ufuna ukuvimbela izikhangiso, abathrekhi kanye nama-malwares ngokuphumelelayo, uma ufuna ukulondoloza uhlelo lwakho lwedatha, yenza idivayisi yakho isebenze ngokushesha futhi uvikele izimfihlo zakho ohlelweni olulodwa, i-Blokada ihamba phambili. Imahhala, iphephile futhi umthombo ovulekile.

I-Blokada isebenza kuzo zonke izinhlelo zakho, hhayi i-inthanethi kuphela! Ayidinge ushintsho lwedivayisi, isekela kokubili i-wifi namanethiwekhi weselula, futhi iphephile ngokuphelele futhi ilula ukuyisebenzisa. Vele uyivule, yona izosebenza ngokuthula!

Indaba yethu

I-Blokada yaqala ukukhishwa ngoLwezi 2016 ngu-Karol Gusak unjiniyela. Umgomo kwakuwukugcina amadivaysi akhululekile futhi agweme okuqukethiwe okungadingeki okusebenzisa idatha yeselula ngokweqile. Izici zokuqala nezilungiso zithunyelwe masinyane, isihloko sohlelo kwiforamu elidumile le-XDA sasinezibuyekezo zehora. Ukuze itholakale kalula kubasebenzisi futhi ibasize kalula, iphrojekthi yavula indlela youxhumana kwi-Telegram. Amalunga amabili okuqala kwakunguKarol kanye nelinye ilunga lesigungu, uPéter Róth. Kamuva waqala ukuvuselela ikhasi le-Facebook ne-website yevimba sikhangiso, ngaphandle kwalokho waze wasiza ekamelweni lengxoxo elikhula ngokushesha.

Ukwakha iqembu eliza ukuhumusha uhlelo, lakhe okuqukethiwe, ukuhlola nokuguqula izinguqulo ze-beta nokuphatha amakhasi omphakathi, I-Blokada Insiders yasungulwa ngo-2018 futhi isebenza ngenkuthalo ukwenza i-Blokada ibe impumelelo eyiyona. Ubuwazi ukuthi manje i-Blokada itholakala ngezilimu ezingu-25?
USebastian Januszevski wajoyina ukuphathwa kwesiteshi seTelegram, esiza amalunga futhi efaka isandla ekuhloleni uhlelo, nokukhiqiza imibiko yabasebenzisi. Ngokusekelwe kokukhathazeka kwakhe nolwazi, i-Blokada yaqina futhi yithembekile.

UKarol wazalwa e-Poland. Ngemva kokuchitha iminyaka eminingana eSweden, usebuyele eWarsaw, lapho ehlanganyela khona emisebenzini ehlukahlukene yeselula njengomthuthukisi. Awumangele, angithi?
UPéter uvela eHungary, esiseduze nenhloko-dolobha yezwe, iBudapest, lapho asebenza njengonjiniyela emkhakheni we-IT, ekwazi ukujula kobuchwepheshe be-Big Data, esekela izinkampani emhlabeni wonke ezaziwayo ukuze zifeze umgomo wazo.
U-Sebastian uhlala futhi usebenza eBuenos Aires lapho afundisa khona izibalo kanye nokucutshungulwa kwedatha kwinyuvesi. Ungumthuthukisi wokuzilibazisa ku-PHP ne-JavaScript, futhi kukhishwe ama-plugin ambalwa we-WordPress ngaphansi kwegama lakhe.

Kusukela ekuqaleni, i-Blokada ilayishwe futhi ifakwe kumadivayisi angaphezu kuka-200.000, kusukela kumafoni kuya kumathebhulethi nakuma-smart TV.

Sithini isibonakalo sethu? Ithimba lizosebenza ukusekela ububanzi bamadivayisi, lenze kube lula ukuxhuma kunsizakalo yangempela ye-VPN ngenkathi i-Blokada isebenza futhi ivikela ubumfihlo bakho ngaphezulu, ikunike ulwazi oluningi mayelana nezikhangiso ozivikelwe kuzo.
